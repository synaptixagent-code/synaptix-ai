FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does setup take?

About 15 minutes total, and you do almost none of the work. Here's what happens: We get on a quick call, you tell us where your leads come from (website form, Google Ads, Facebook, etc.), and we connect to those sources on our end. Then we ask you a few questions about your business — what services you offer, your service area, your tone — so we can write response templates that sound like you. You review the templates, approve them, and you're live. Most business owners are surprised it's that simple. There's no software to install, no passwords to remember, and no training required.

Will it sound like a robot?

Not at all — and this is usually the first thing people ask. We don't send generic template messages. During setup, we learn how you actually talk to customers and we craft responses that match your voice. For example, if you run an HVAC company, a response might read: "Hi Sarah, thanks for reaching out to Johnson Heating and Air about the AC issue at your Catonsville home. I've got availability later this week and can come take a look. Would Thursday afternoon work for you?" That sounds like a real person because it's written to sound like YOU. Every message includes the customer's name, what they asked about, and a clear next step. Your customers will think it's you or your office manager texting back quickly — not a computer.

What if I want to stop?

You cancel with a single email or phone call — no hoops, no retention calls, no guilt trips. There's no contract. There's no cancellation fee. There's no minimum commitment. Your first month is completely free, so there's literally zero financial risk. If at any point you decide it's not for you, you tell us and we shut it off the same day. We disconnect from your lead sources, and everything goes back to exactly how it was before. We'll even send you a summary of all the leads we handled so you don't lose any data. We built it this way on purpose — we'd rather earn your business every month than trap you in a contract.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

If you can send a text message and check email, you have all the technical skills you need. Seriously. We set up everything for you — you don't log into any software, you don't install any apps, and you don't configure anything. After setup, the system runs completely on its own. You'll get a link to a simple dashboard where you can see your leads if you want to, but you don't have to check it. Most of our customers are tradespeople who spend their day with tools in their hands, not sitting at a computer. That's exactly who we built this for. If something ever needs to change — like you want to update your service area or adjust a response template — you just tell us and we handle it.

How is this different from a CRM?

A CRM is a tool that helps you organize and manage leads — but it still requires you to log in, read each lead, and manually respond to them. That's the part most contractors never actually do consistently. You buy the CRM, you use it for two weeks, and then it becomes an expensive address book. Synaptix AI is different because it does the work for you. It doesn't wait for you to log in. The second a lead comes in, it responds automatically with a personalized message. Then it follows up on Day 3 and Day 7 if the lead doesn't respond. You don't have to open an app, remember to check it, or learn any new software. Think of it this way: a CRM is like a filing cabinet for your leads. Synaptix AI is like hiring a full-time receptionist who never takes a break, responds instantly, and costs a fraction of what you'd pay a person.
